ABOUT US

With over 8 years of experience in home and building automation in U.A.E., Smart Home and Building Control Systems is now set to expand its expertise in the Indian market by looking into the modern living and growing technology of the region

Smart Home and Building Control Systems work in-line with green building technology where we strive to give the best possible solution to save energy and reduce carbon footprint enabling the users to live with modern technology in a more luxurious and flexible way. Smart home and building automation system will understand consumer's requirements and will help to provide customized cost effective solutions with savings in the electrical energy and an increase in the property's market value.
